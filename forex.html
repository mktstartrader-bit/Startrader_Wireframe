A forex broker is a company that gives traders access to trading platforms where they can buy and sell currency pairs online and benefit from price movements.

Spread is the difference between the ask and bid price. A zero- or low-spread broker keeps that gap minimal, which can help traders maximise potential returns.

Spreads change with the market, so no broker can guarantee zero at all times, and some charge commissions, withdrawal or inactivity fees. STARTRADER charges 0 commission and 0 deposit fees on most channels.

Check the broker’s website for its regulatory status and licenses from reputable authorities. STARTRADER holds licenses from ASIC (Australia), the FSCA (South Africa) and the FSA (Seychelles).

Traders aim to profit from changes in currency-pair prices by buying and selling on the 24/5 foreign-exchange market. Trades use lots — a standard lot is 100,000 units, a mini lot 10,000 and a micro lot 1,000 — and leverage lets you trade a larger amount with less capital.

MT4 and MT5 are the most popular, offering advanced charting, customisable indicators and expert advisors to automate trades.

Macroeconomic data such as inflation, other capital markets, international trade figures and political news all significantly influence daily forex volume.
